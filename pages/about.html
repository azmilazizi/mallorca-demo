  • MALLORCA INTERNATIONAL

  • Mallorca Rim is an integrated amusement project which combined tourism, sport tourism, education tourism and edutainment. The project will comprised 6 major component which are 21 unit of high end roller coaster (600 acres), theme park (300 acres), water theme park (282 acres), adventure park (294 acres), international tennis centre (111 acres) and commercial centre and hotel (300 acres) to be developed by Althamis Amusement (Alor Gajah) Sdn Bhd. Located in the State of Melaka , the project is expected to draw 660 million attendance from all over Asean countries. The project is expected to complete by June, 2026

    • STATUS OF THE PROJECT

  • The project is at the final stages of approvals and award by the Ministry of Works (Kementerian Kerja Raya) to Sungai Klang Link Sdn Bhd (SKL), the Special Vehicle tasked to execute this project. Having being conceived as early as 2017, the project has undergone many stages of planning to design and proposals. The development of the highway impacts many parties. SKL has been actively engaging with all key stakeholders, ranging from the pertinent minitries and local authorities. Since the project involves the rehabilitation and preservation of the Klang river and its surrounding areas, the project has been endorsed by the Selangor government, as part of the Selangor Maritime Gateway initiative. We believe our holistic approach in the development process and the constant engagement with the stakeholders has given us the buy in to execute the project.
